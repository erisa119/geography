Experience the Hidden Gem of Europe

Discover the untouched beauty of South Albania, where pristine beaches meet ancient history and authentic Mediterranean culture.

Why South Albania?

South Albania offers a perfect blend of Mediterranean beauty, rich history, and authentic culture. From UNESCO World Heritage sites to pristine beaches and mouthwatering cuisine, this undiscovered paradise has something for every traveler.

Rich Culture

Experience centuries-old traditions, authentic festivals, and warm hospitality from locals who welcome visitors with open arms.

Stunning Nature

From the crystal-clear waters of the Albanian Riviera to the dramatic mountains and pristine national parks, nature lovers will be in paradise.

Delicious Cuisine

Savor the flavors of authentic Mediterranean cuisine with fresh seafood, organic produce, and traditional recipes passed down through generations.

Discover Southern Cities

Explore the charming cities and towns of South Albania, each with its own unique character, history, and attractions.

Vlora

Where the Adriatic meets the Ionian Sea

Albania's second largest port city offers beautiful beaches, rich history, and serves as the perfect gateway to the Albanian Riviera.

Saranda

The pearl of the Albanian Riviera

This coastal gem offers stunning beaches, vibrant nightlife, and easy access to ancient ruins, including the UNESCO World Heritage site of Butrint.

Gjirokastra

The city of stone

This UNESCO World Heritage site features unique Ottoman architecture with stone houses, a magnificent castle, and rich cultural heritage.

Himara

Coastal paradise with Greek heritage

A charming coastal town with beautiful beaches, traditional villages, and a unique blend of Albanian and Greek cultural influences.

Berat

The city of a thousand windows

This UNESCO World Heritage site features a well-preserved Ottoman old town with white houses cascading down the hillside and a magnificent castle.

Permet

The city of roses and thermal springs

Known for its natural thermal baths, stunning mountain scenery, and traditional Albanian cuisine, Permet offers an authentic rural experience.

Discover the most breathtaking and culturally significant sites across South Albania.

UNESCO Site

An ancient city that was once part of Greek, Roman, Byzantine and Venetian empires, now a UNESCO World Heritage site with remarkably preserved ruins.

Blue Eye (Syri i Kaltër)

Natural Wonder

A hypnotic natural spring where crystal clear water bubbles from a deep blue pool, surrounded by lush vegetation and pristine nature.

Gjirokastra Castle

Gjirokastra

Historical

One of the largest castles in the Balkans, offering panoramic views of the city and housing a fascinating military museum with artifacts from different eras.

Ksamil Islands

Beach Paradise

A group of small islands with white sandy beaches and crystal-clear turquoise waters, often compared to the Caribbean for their stunning beauty.
